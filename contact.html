Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum capacity of your venue?

Our grand ballroom can accommodate up to 200 guests for a seated dinner, and up to 300 for a cocktail-style reception. For outdoor events, our gardens can host up to 250 guests.

Is there on-site parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site parking for up to 150 vehicles. We also provide valet parking services for special events and can arrange shuttle services from nearby hotels if needed.

Can we use our own vendors?

We have a preferred vendor list that we're happy to share, but you're welcome to bring in your own vendors. We just ask that they provide proof of insurance and meet our venue requirements.

Do you offer all-inclusive packages?

Yes, we offer several all-inclusive packages that include venue rental, catering, basic decor, and event coordination. We can also create custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that circumstances can change. Our cancellation policy allows for full refunds up to 90 days before your event, with partial refunds available up to 30 days before. We're always happy to work with you to reschedule if possible.

Do you offer tours of the venue?

Absolutely! We encourage all potential clients to visit The Grand Elm in person. We offer tours by appointment during our office hours, and we're happy to accommodate evening and weekend tours for special circumstances.
