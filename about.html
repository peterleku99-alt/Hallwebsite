About The Grand Elm

Discover the story behind our historic venue and meet the passionate team dedicated to creating your perfect event.

Our Story

The Grand Elm began its journey in 1892 as the magnificent estate of the prestigious Elmwood family. Originally built as a summer retreat, this architectural gem has witnessed over a century of celebrations, from intimate family gatherings to grand society events.

In 2015, after years of careful restoration and modernization, The Grand Elm opened its doors as a premier event venue. Our restoration team worked tirelessly to preserve the building's historic character while adding modern amenities that today's events demand.

Today, The Grand Elm stands as a testament to timeless elegance, offering the perfect blend of historic charm and contemporary luxury. Every corner of our venue tells a story, and we invite you to become part of our continuing legacy.

Our Philosophy

At The Grand Elm, we believe that every event is unique and deserves to be treated with the same care and attention to detail that we would give our own celebrations. Our philosophy is simple: create an environment where memories are made naturally, where every detail enhances the experience, and where our guests feel truly special.

We understand that planning an event can be overwhelming, which is why we've assembled a team of experts who are passionate about making your vision a reality. From the initial consultation to the final farewell, we're here to ensure that your event exceeds your expectations.
